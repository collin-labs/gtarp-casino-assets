**Framer Motion roda:**
- **Idle:** `animate={{ rotate: [0, 2, -2, 0] }}` `transition={{ duration: 6, repeat: Infinity, ease: 'easeInOut' }}`
- **Spinning:** `animate={{ rotate: finalAngle }}` `transition={{ duration: 4 + Math.random() * 1.5, ease: [0.15, 0.85, 0.35, 1.0] }}`
- **Resultado:** sem animacao (parada no angulo final)

**Framer Motion bola:**
- **Idle:** nenhum (posicao estatica topo)
- **Spinning:** `animate={{ rotate: [0, -1080, -1800, -2100, -2160] }}` com `times: [0, 0.3, 0.7, 0.9, 1]`
- **Bounce final:** `animate={{ y: [0, 2, -1, 0] }}` `transition={{ duration: 0.3, delay: spinDuration * 0.9 }}`
