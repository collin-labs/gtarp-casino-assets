(8) Paytable US padrao: Royal Flush 100:1 ate Pair 1:1. (9) 50.1% das maos de 5 cartas sao High Card (fold frequente). (10) Ambos modos compartilham 90% do codigo (deck, shuffle, card rendering, chip animations).
